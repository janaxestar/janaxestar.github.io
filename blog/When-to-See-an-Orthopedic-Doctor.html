When to See an Orthopedic Doctor?

Many people ignore joint pain or injuries until they become severe. Early consultation can prevent complications.

Signs You Should Not Ignore

  • Persistent joint pain
  • Swelling or stiffness
  • Difficulty walking
  • Sports injuries

Why Early Treatment Matters

Timely diagnosis can prevent surgery and long-term damage.
