Knee Pain Treatment in Visakhapatnam – Causes & Best Solutions

Knee pain is one of the most common orthopedic problems affecting people of all ages in Visakhapatnam. It can range from mild discomfort to severe pain that limits daily activities.

Common Causes of Knee Pain

  • Osteoarthritis (age-related wear and tear)
  • Ligament injuries (ACL, PCL tears)
  • Meniscus tears
  • Sports injuries

When Should You See an Orthopedic Doctor?

If knee pain persists for more than 2 weeks, causes swelling, or affects walking, consult a specialist.

Best Treatment Options

  • Medications and physiotherapy
  • Injections for pain relief
  • Arthroscopy (keyhole surgery)
  • Knee replacement surgery (for severe cases)
